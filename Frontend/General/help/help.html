For Student

Search for jobs and view job details
  1. Go to the Homepage.
  2. Enter a keyword in the search box or use filters (location/field/experience/salary, etc.).
  3. Click the search button or press Enter to search for jobs.
  4. Click a job to view the details and the company’s basic information.
Create your own CV based on available templates
  1. Click My CV on the header menu.
  2. If you have not created a CV yet, fill in your information to create one. If you have created a CV before, your previous CV will be displayed.
  3. You can choose Edit CV to update it or Download CV to download it as a PDF.
Apply for a job
  1. Open the job details page and click "Apply now".
  2. Select a CV file from your computer.
  3. Click “Apply” to submit your application.

For Employer

Update company profile (required before posting jobs)
  1. Go to Edit Profile.
  2. Fill in all required company information.
  3. Add a company avatar/logo (it will be shown on job posts).
Post a job
  1. Go to Post Job.
  2. Enter job information (title, field, salary, location, experience, etc.).
  3. Click Publish/Post to create the post.
Manage job posts
  • View the list of job posts.
  • Edit/Delete/Update the status of job posts.
  • There is also a manage candidates for each post by press the button
Manage candidates (applications/CVs)
  1. Go to Manage Post Job.
  2. Click the blue button on each post to see the candidate
  3. View the list of candidates for each job.
  4. Open a candidate's CV to view details.
Notifications
  • View notifications when there is a new application / status changes (if supported by the system).
